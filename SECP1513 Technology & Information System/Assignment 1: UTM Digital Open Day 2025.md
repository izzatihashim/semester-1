## üí° Summary
### üó£ ‚Äî Talks and sharing sessions
- **Talk 1: Information System** <br>
-> Speaker: Hj. Madradzi bin Muhammad, Senior IT Officer <br>
-> This talk highlighted about the Information System in UTM, specifically at UTM Digital, that plays a major role in serving the digital services at UTM. I have learnt about UTM Digital and how they are implemented in the management system such as UTM Smart as well as the behind process of developing it. He also shared about UI/UX which is a User-Centred Design that is used in UTM Smart and blockchain technology which is a secure ecosystem & environment. Not only that, he also talked about the emerging technologies of AI and how its implementation in UTM Servies, such as AI photo validator and a mini chat bot called FlowiseAI.

- **Talk 2: Digital Infra** <br>
-> Speaker: Mr. Mohd Hafiz bin Muhammad Nasir, Senior Assistant IT Officer <br>
-> This second talk are mostly centred about the UTM Data Center, whereas Mr. Hafiz showed us where and how the Data Center at UTM is operated. It is inspiring to see the real world example of things that I had learnt in class, it inspired me to explore the digital world further. In addition to that, he also discussed about the IT Infrastucture, IT components and several tiers in data.

- **Talk 3: Data Engineering** <br>
-> Speaker: Mr. Zahari bin Supene, Assistant IT Officer <br>
-> Mr. Zahari delivered a very transparent sharing about the importance and real definitions of data engineering. Not only that, I was also able to learn on how  UTM data flows and handled along with its actual product, that will function to support data-driven decision in the organisation. This talk felt closest to me as it can relates to my field of study and gave me a clearer view of my future prospects.

- **Talk 4: System Development** <br>
-> Speaker: Mr. Helmee bin Yaacob, Senior IT Officer <br>
-> This talk about software development and project management gave me some new insights and experiences that can surely help me in my future career. He taught us how to ensure a successful project, a proper system development process and development tools that support UTM Digital services. Not only that, he also shared about several practical methods into approaching a successful project development.
